The Process

Four Steps. Zero Guesswork.

Every returned device follows the same precise, auditable pipeline.

Step 01

Ingest & Identify

Devices are placed on the intake tray and automatically scanned. Within seconds, the system pulls device identity and status data — no manual data entry required.

  • IMEI / serial number scan
  • Model & SKU identification
  • Carrier lock & activation status
  • iCloud / FRP lock detection
  • Blacklist & stolen device check
🔍
Step 02

Robotic Inspection

The grading cell performs a comprehensive physical and functional inspection using high-resolution cameras, pressure sensors, and diagnostic probes.

  • 360-degree cosmetic imaging
  • Screen defect detection (cracks, burn-in, dead pixels)
  • Battery health & cycle count
  • Camera, speaker, mic testing
  • Water damage indicator check
  • Button & port functionality
🤖
Step 03

AI-Powered Grading

Inspection results are fed into our grading models, which assign a consistent, auditable grade based on your custom grading matrix. Every decision is logged and traceable.

  • Custom grading matrices per client
  • Consistent grades — no human variance
  • Fraud detection (swapped parts, refurb disguised as new)
  • Full audit trail for every device
  • Grade confidence scoring
🧠
Step 04

Smart Routing

Based on the grade, market value, and your business rules, each device is instantly routed to its highest-value disposition channel.

  • Refurbish — high-value devices worth repairing
  • Resell as-is — grade A/B units to secondary markets
  • Part-out — harvest valuable components
  • Recycle — responsible e-waste processing
  • Real-time routing dashboard
📊
